About

Hi, I'm Emerentia Novena.
I've always been driven by curiosity and a love for learning, which has taken me across disciplines and continents — from studying Business Marketing in Canada, to exploring Languages and Literature in Taiwan, and now diving into Information Technology in Scotland. While my current studies are in IT, I’m passionate about combining my background in communication, culture, and technology to create user-centered digital experiences. I don’t fit neatly into one box — and that’s my strength. I bring a global perspective, interdisciplinary thinking, and a deep curiosity to everything I do. I’m still exploring exactly where I’ll land, but I know I want to build things that help people connect, learn, and feel understood.
