Story-Based Language Learning Web App

This interactive language-learning platform immerses users in branching stories that adapt to their choices. Each decision introduces new vocabulary in a natural, contextual way—blending storytelling with education to create an intuitive and emotionally engaging experience.

Key Features

  • • Interactive branching storylines for immersive learning
  • • Progress tracking
  • • Multi-language support (expandable)

Design Tools Used

Figma:UX flows, wireframes, visual design
InkleWriter: Story logic prototyping

Outcome & Impact

The app successfully provides an engaging and effective alternative to traditional language learning methods. Early user feedback indicates higher retention rates and increased motivation due to the interactive and story-driven approach. It has proven particularly effective for learners who struggle with conventional rote memorisation techniques.
